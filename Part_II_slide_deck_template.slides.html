by Reem Alshehri¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the characteristics of Loans that could be used to predict the future loans features. The main focus was on the Borrower's Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for the loan, The Borrower's interest rate for this loan and the most reasons for people to take loans (listing categories).

Dataset Overview¶

This document explores The Prosper Loan data set that contains 113,937 loans with 81 features, including loan amount, borrower rate (or interest rate), current loan status, borrower income, and many others,i have chosen 15 columns of interest to do my Investigation.
